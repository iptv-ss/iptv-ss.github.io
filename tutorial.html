StarShare IPTV Tutorial

Learn how to set up your IPTV service on various devices with detailed steps.

Step 1: Download the App

Download the IPTV Smarters Pro app from the official website or app store.

Step 2: Install the App

Install the app on your device following the standard installation procedure.

Step 3: Open the App

Launch the IPTV Smarters Pro app from your device's application menu.

Step 4: Accept Terms

Read and accept the terms and conditions to proceed.

Step 5: Add New User

Select "Add New User" to set up your IPTV account.

Step 6: Enter Login Details

Enter your username, password, and server URL provided by StarShare IPTV.

Step 7: Click Add User

Click on "Add User" to save your account details.

Step 8: Enjoy Streaming

Start enjoying over 10,000 channels and 60,000 movies and series.
